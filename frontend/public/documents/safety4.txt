Kochi Metro Ridership Analysis – September 2025

Total ridership for September reached 3.2 million passengers, a 6% increase from August. Weekday traffic averaged 120,000 passengers per day, while weekend ridership averaged 85,000 passengers. Peak hours were observed between 8:00–10:00 AM and 5:30–7:30 PM. Most passengers traveled between Aluva and MG Road, followed by Edapally to Palarivattom.

Survey data indicated that 70% of riders are daily commuters, 20% students, and 10% occasional travelers. Passenger satisfaction scores were 4.5/5 for punctuality, 4.2/5 for cleanliness, and 4.0/5 for accessibility. Recommendations include increasing train frequency during peak hours and enhancing last-mile connectivity through feeder buses and bicycle rental services.
