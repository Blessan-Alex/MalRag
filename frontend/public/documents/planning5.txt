The metro authority is planning the extension of Line 1 from Petta to Tripunithura, adding approximately 12 km and 8 new stations. Feasibility studies project a 40% ridership increase after the extension. Land acquisition and environmental clearance processes are underway. Budget estimates for construction and operations are projected at INR 1800 Crores.

Integration with upcoming metro lines, bus rapid transit corridors, and water metro services is being explored. Sustainable infrastructure measures, including solar-powered stations, rainwater harvesting, and green landscaping, are prioritized. A phased implementation is suggested, focusing on sections with the highest commuter demand. Stakeholder consultations, including community feedback and safety assessments, are integral to planning and execution.
